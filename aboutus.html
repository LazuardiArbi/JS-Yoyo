History

Js yoyo is a yoyo store that was established in 2007. And was founded by two young men who originally sold yo-yo because they love yo-yo toys.

Js yoyo initially sells yoyo not through the store. but with the proliferation of technology, js yoyo has a store in 2014 and finally opened the catalog website to show yoyo collection owned by js yoyo.

This website is dedicated to show the collection from basic to professional yoyo, and provide tutorial how to play a good yoyo.
