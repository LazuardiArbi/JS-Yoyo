How to Play Yo-yo

1. Hold the yo-yo in your hand, palm up.

2. Throw the yo-yo forward and downward, opening with your hand open wide and facing your palm outward.

3. Turn your hand over so thath your palm is facing the floor and sharply tug the string.

4. Catch the yo-yo in the palm of your hand, turn it over and repeat
